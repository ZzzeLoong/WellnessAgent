# Muscle Gain Nutrition Basics

Muscle gain planning should support training, recovery, and consistent protein intake.

## Common guidance

- Eat enough total energy to support training performance and recovery.
- Distribute protein across multiple meals during the day.
- Combine resistance training with a nutrition plan instead of relying on food alone.
- Include carbohydrate around training sessions to support performance when appropriate.

## Meal template

- Breakfast: protein plus carbohydrate, such as eggs with oats or tofu with rice
- Lunch: a larger mixed meal with protein, grains, and vegetables
- Snack: yogurt, soy yogurt, fruit, sandwich, milk, or protein-rich snack
- Dinner: protein-rich main dish with starch and vegetables

## Coaching note

If the user's goal is lean muscle gain, avoid excessive surplus and keep food quality high. Track digestion, appetite, training energy, and weight trend over time.

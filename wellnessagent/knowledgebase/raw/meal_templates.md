# Meal Planning Templates

Simple meal templates help turn goals and constraints into practical daily plans.

## Breakfast templates

- Greek yogurt or soy yogurt, fruit, oats, and seeds
- Eggs or tofu scramble with toast and vegetables
- Oatmeal with milk or soy milk, fruit, and nuts

## Lunch templates

- Rice bowl with tofu or chicken, mixed vegetables, and sauce that fits the user's restrictions
- Salad bowl with beans or eggs, grains, and olive-oil-based dressing
- Sandwich or wrap with protein, vegetables, and fruit on the side

## Dinner templates

- Stir-fry with protein, vegetables, and rice
- Soup or stew with beans, vegetables, and potatoes
- Grain bowl with salmon, tofu, or legumes plus cooked vegetables

## Snack templates

- Fruit plus yogurt
- Edamame
- Roasted chickpeas
- Nuts or seeds when allergy-safe
- Protein shake when needed and tolerated

## Planning rule

Prefer meal templates that match the user's cooking time, budget, food access, and dietary restrictions.
